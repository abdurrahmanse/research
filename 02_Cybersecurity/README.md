Cybersecurity Domain

Purpose: Threat detection, anomaly detection, incident analysis, and policy reports.

Suggested subfolders:

- `projects/` : experiments, detection pipelines, and playbooks
- `datasets/` : network/host telemetry and labeled attack datasets
- `papers/` : research papers and threat reports
- `reports/` : incident summaries, policy briefs, and compliance notes
- `notebooks/` : analysis and visualization notebooks

Tip: separate sensitive logs from research artifacts and document retention policies.
